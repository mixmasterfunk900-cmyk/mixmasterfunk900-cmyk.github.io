♟️ Chess Uploader

Personal content automation for Road to 1000 ELO

Chess Uploader is a personal content-automation tool built for a single chess creator ("Chess-Man", Road to 1000 ELO). It renders the creator's own recorded chess gameplay into long-form videos and short clips, then uploads them to the creator's own connected TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API.

  • Only the account owner's own original gameplay footage is processed.
  • Videos are delivered to the creator's TikTok account for review and posting.
  • No third-party or user-generated content is involved.

Signs in with TikTok (Login Kit) and authorizes this app to upload the creator's own clips to their account.
